Angulal.io and A-frame:

What is a-frame ?

Mostly a-frame is:

  • Amazing
  • Awesome
  • Jawdropping

A-frame is a great library build on three.js designed to allow easy creating virtual reality apps and websites.

But..why ?

A-frame based sites are supported by most browsers supporting html5 (only found issues with html video tags on the apple systems), yet the virtual reality can be experienced on:

  • STEAM's DESKTOP VR:
    • HTC VIVE
    • OCULUS RIFT
  • Modern Smartphones with a cardboard or GEAR-like equipment
